Never used China Edition Monopoly Game with sealed pieces, cards, and money. This China Edition Monopoly is in like new condition with sealed pieces, cards, and money. The Monopoly game has never been used and the box was only opened to inspect the contents. This Monopoly game was distributed by Hasbro Hong Kong and was only available in China. The game is bilingual in both Chinese and English. The Monopoly is complete with China Edition game board, 8 bronzed pewter tokens: Streetcar, Trophy, Race Car, Elephant, Shoe, Bathtub, Candlestick, and Mr. Monopoly, Chinese cities title deed property cards, Monopoly money, banker's tray, 16 Community Chest cards, 16 Chance cards, 32 green Houses, 12 red Hotels, 2 red and white dice, and instructions. The box is in excellent condition with just a very slight indent on the bottom. The China Edition Monopoly was made in 2001. From a smoke free home and owned by a collector.
